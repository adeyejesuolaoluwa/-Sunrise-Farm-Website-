Animal Health & Care

Expert guidance on poultry health and wellness

Common Poultry Illnesses

🦠

Coccidiosis

A parasitic disease affecting the intestines

Symptoms: Blood in stool, weight loss, lethargy

Treatment: Coccidiostats or amprolium

🤧

Newcastle Disease

A highly contagious viral infection

Symptoms: Respiratory issues, neurological signs

Treatment: Vaccination (prevention), supportive care

🦗

Worm Infestation

Internal parasites affecting nutrient absorption

Symptoms: Poor growth, dull feathers, weakness

Treatment: Anthelmintics (deworming agents)

🔥

Fowl Pox

A viral disease causing skin lesions

Symptoms: Scabs on skin, reduced egg production

Treatment: Vaccination (prevention), isolation

Medications & Treatments

Medication Type Purpose Usage
Tetracycline Antibiotic Bacterial infections Oral or injectable
Amprolium Anticoccidial Coccidiosis prevention/treatment Water-soluble
Levamisole Anthelmintic Internal worm parasites Oral or injectable
Vitamin Supplements Supplement Immune system support Water or feed additive
Newcastle Vaccine Vaccine Newcastle disease prevention Injectable or oral
Marek's Vaccine Vaccine Marek's disease prevention Day-old injection
⚠️

Important Safety Information

Always consult a licensed veterinarian before administering any medications or treatments to your poultry.

Improper use of medications can lead to resistance, residues in meat/eggs, and harm to animal welfare. Our team recommends regular veterinary checkups and preventive care practices.
